Rupa & Company Ltd. is one of the leading Knitwear Manufacturing Companies in India with an enviable portfolio comprising of popular brands like Frontline, Softline, Euro, Bumchums, Torrido, Thermocot, Footline and Jon, covering an entire range of knitted garments from Inner Wear to Casual Wear, Sleep Wear to Thermal Wear.
RUPA brand was launched in 1968 in order to give shape to the vision of Mr P R Agarwala and his brothers, Mr G P Agarwala and Mr K B Agarwala, of establishing an indigenous knitwear brand that would cater to the daily inner wear needs of men, women and kids alike. The Company has come a long way from its humble beginnings in the bylanes of Kolkata (then Calcutta) to become a fashion-forward brand with a strong brand presence across the country. The Company is also moving rapidly to make its presence felt in the international arena, especially in GCC, Africa and Asia Pacific.
RUPA is among the largest knitwear manufacturers in India with its manufacturing facilities situated in Bangalore, Tirupur, Kolkata and Ghaziabad.
Headquartered in Kolkata, the Company has its sales offices located at Patna, Varanasi, Mumbai, Jaipur, Indore, Delhi, Ghaziabad, Kanpur, Bangalore, Tirupur, and Hyderabad. Rupa & Company Ltd. is also accredited of having one of the largest distribution networks across India with over 100,000 retailers and more than 1,000 dealers. RUPA endorses ISO 9001:2008 Certification and is listed on NSE, and BSE.
